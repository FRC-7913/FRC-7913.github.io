FRC.Team(7913)

Team 7913, Bear'ly Functioning, is the robotics team of Newfound Regional High School in Bristol, New Hampshire. We compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition, an international engineering experience for high schoolers. The team has existed since 2019 and has competed in the New England District Championship.
